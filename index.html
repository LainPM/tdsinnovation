Our Story

Tradition Since 1895

Pranav Bakery was founded in 1895 in the heart of Mumbai. For generations, we have been baking with the same passion and recipes handed down through our family. Our bakery is a living piece of Mumbai's history, beloved by locals and visitors alike.

We believe in honest ingredients, time-honored methods, and the joy of sharing fresh bread and sweets with our community. Step into our bakery and experience a taste of old Mumbai!
