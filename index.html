About TDS Innovation

TDS Innovation was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make mental health support accessible to every young person in India, regardless of their ability to pay or their comfort with traditional therapy settings.

We believe that everyone deserves compassionate support for their emotional wellbeing, and that mental health resources should be culturally relevant, stigma-free, and available when people need them most.
