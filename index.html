Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the virtual try-ons?
Tryona achieves 92% accuracy in fit visualization based on independent testing. Our AI accounts for fabric drape, body proportions, and garment sizing. Colors may vary slightly due to screen differences.
What photo requirements are there?
For best results: Full-body photo with feet visible, arms slightly away from body, form-fitting clothing, plain background, and good lighting. The app will guide you through the process.
Can I try on my own clothes?
Absolutely! Upload clear images of your clothing items (front and back views recommended) to add them to your virtual wardrobe. Our AI will map them to your body.
Is my data private and secure?
Completely. We use military-grade encryption and never share your photos or data without permission. You can delete all data anytime through the app settings.
Which brands are available?
We partner with 200+ major brands across categories. The app is constantly adding new partners. You can also try on items from any store by uploading product images.
