Frequently Asked Questions

Essentials include:

  • 2-3 liters of water
  • Energy snacks and lunch
  • Good quality trekking shoes
  • First-aid kit
  • Raincoat/poncho (in monsoon)
  • Extra pair of clothes
  • Torch with extra batteries
  • Personal medications

We have treks for all levels:

  • Beginner: Sinhagad, Lohagad, Tikona
  • Intermediate: Rajgad, Torna, Purandar
  • Advanced: Harishchandragad, Kalsubai, Sandhan Valley

Our guides will assess your fitness level and recommend appropriate treks.

Cancellation charges:

  • 15+ days before trek: Full refund
  • 7-14 days before: 50% refund
  • Less than 7 days: No refund

In case of bad weather, we either reschedule or offer full refund.

Our Story

Founded in 2011 by a group of passionate trekkers, Trekkers of India has grown from a small hiking club to one of India's most respected trekking communities. What began as weekend trips to nearby forts has evolved into organized expeditions across the country.

We believe in responsible trekking that respects both nature and history. Our treks combine physical challenge with cultural education, creating experiences that stay with you long after you've descended the mountain.

Our Mission

To make trekking accessible to everyone while preserving India's natural and historical heritage. We aim to:

  • Promote outdoor activities and healthy lifestyles
  • Educate trekkers about historical sites and conservation
  • Build a supportive community of adventure enthusiasts
  • Organize safe, well-planned treks for all skill levels
  • Document and preserve lesser-known trails and routes
