Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kadam Farms suitable for families with children?

Absolutely! We have special activities designed for kids and families.

Do you provide vegetarian or vegan food?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan options. Let us know your preferences in advance.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer free and secure parking on the premises.

How can I book my stay?

Simply fill out the contact form or call us directly at the number provided on the Contact page.
