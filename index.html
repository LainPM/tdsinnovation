About Our Farm

Discover Our Peaceful Retreat

Nestled in the picturesque countryside of Phaltan, Satara, Kadam Farms offers a perfect blend of rural charm and modern comfort. Our farmhouse spans over 5 acres of lush greenery, providing a tranquil escape from city life.

Established in 2010, we've been hosting guests who seek authentic farm experiences, organic food, and a chance to reconnect with nature. Our farm produces seasonal fruits, vegetables, and dairy products that you can enjoy during your stay.

Whether you're looking for a weekend getaway, a family vacation, or a venue for special events, Kadam Farms provides the perfect setting with its serene atmosphere and warm hospitality.

Our Facilities

Comfortable Cottages

Stay in our well-appointed cottages with modern amenities while enjoying rustic charm. Each cottage has a private sit-out area with farm views.

Organic Dining

Enjoy farm-to-table meals prepared with ingredients harvested from our organic farm. Special dietary requirements can be accommodated.

Farm Activities

Engage in milking cows, fruit picking, pottery, or traditional cooking classes. We also offer bullock cart rides and guided farm tours.
