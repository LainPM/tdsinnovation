About TDS Innovation

TDS Innovation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to making mental health support accessible to everyone in India. We believe that mental healthcare should be free, culturally sensitive, and available when people need it most.

Our team includes mental health professionals, counselors, and volunteers who are passionate about breaking the stigma around mental health in Indian society.
